About Us

Clearcode Technologies Private Limited (CCTPL)

Clearcode Technologies Private Limited (CCTPL)is registered under The Companies Act 2013. The company nature is IT and ITS (Information Technology and enabled services). Clearcode technology has very clear and broad vision to provide the IT Services for the people, Agriculture, Retail, Education, Banking, Manufacturing industries, Chemical industries to make their day to day work simpler, easy and faster. Through the innovative ideas and advanced technology Clearcode want to bring changes in all the above mentioned sectors, Technology always make human life faster, make them save their time and money. Clearcode is working on this and trying to bring technologically revolutions for the society and the people around the world.

CCTPL has already served the companies of different sectors like Agriculture, Environmental groups, Education sector, Retail and trading sectors, Chemical companies, Computer hardware company and banking sectors, we have a long client list where we have provided our services to them with customer satisfaction.
